Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Hiking is the preferred term, in Canada and the United States, for a long, vigorous walk, usually on trails (footpaths).

Kayaking

Kayaking is the use of a kayak for moving across water. It is distinguished from canoeing by the sitting position of the paddler.

Bird Watchings

Birdwatching, or birding, is a form of wildlife observation in which the observation of birds is a recreational activity or citizen science.
